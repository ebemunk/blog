Beholder: Beauty in the eyes of Playboy

An analysis of Playboy's Playmates since 1954. From it's contraversial history to body shapes, we jump into the rabbit hole of this world. Reading it for the articles
Demographics breakdown of the Print (magazine) and Online (playboy.com) versions from 2004. Playboy describes print readers as brand conscious, outdoor enthusiasts, trendsetters and socially active. Online readers are tech savvy, gamers, online shoppers and active & adventurous.
Seeing double?
Some issues featured more than 1 Playmate. All Playmates, except for the first time it happened, share more than the Playmate title. They share DNA! Explore the interactive viz for a deep dive.
Older and wiser
Average age of Playmates increased by almost 20% over the years. Read more about the 7 Playmates who were underage when photographed, and what happened to Hugh Hefner in one case when found out.
The Weighting Game
No Playmate was above 150lbs until 2020. It took 66 years for Playboy to pick a Playmate that's at the US Average for weight. Note how the variance explodes after Hefner's death. 3 outliers tell a story of embracing body acceptance.
Waist circumference
With an 11% increase, waist measurements went up the most over the years. 70% of the Playmates measuring more than 28" came after Hefner's passing. Still, the US average would not fit in this chart. Waist measurement is the farthest from US average from any other measurement.
Hips don't lie
Though it only increased by 2%, this also highlights how the "new Playboy" values Playmates' characteristics differently than Hugh Hefner.
White Man's World
Even though it touts a progressive stance, only 2 most recent years have featured around 40% non-white Playmates. Do you think this mirrors Playboy's demographics?
Real or Fake?
Enhanced breasts came into popularity in the late 80s and fell out of popularity after 2010s.
Playmate Info
An example of what you'd see when clicking on one of the circles in the above visualizations.
Breaking the mold
2020 is the first and only year the hourglass-figure mold was broken. Bust measurements decline through the decades, waist and hips increase. Thicc is finally embraced in 2020. The "Ideal" 36-24-36 shown in red. Projected dimensions of the Barbie doll in yellow.
Straddling the line
More than half of Playmates are Underweight, 48% are in the Healthy range. There are only 2 other Playmates (<1%) in any other category. The difference of the averages between US women and Playmates is staggering.
The Magic Number
A Waist-to-Hip Ratio of 0.7, which accounts for 26% of Playmates is said to be the "perfect" ratio for attractiveness. Highlighted are the "most attractive" WHRs from other cultures/places.
Birthplace of Playmates
Only 18% of Playmates were born outside of the US. Focus on the regions of the world where no Playmate was born in.
Californication
West coast - best coast for Playmates. Vermont and Delaware are the only states that have never had Playmates.
Most/Least Average Playmates
Playmates ordered by their distance to the average of their data points. Old-School vs New-School. Top 5 most average are all from older issues, whereas the top 5 least average are all from 2000 and later.
Difference from Average per Year
Playmates have been increasingly "different" from the "Most Average Playmate" through the years.
k-means for body shape?
Finding 3 clusters for bust, waist and hips measurements. Cluster 1 is for triangle, or "pear" shape. Cluster 2 is square, and Cluster 3 is hourglass.
